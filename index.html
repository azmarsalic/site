About Us

Marawi IT Solutions (ITSMarawi) was recently conceptualized and founded in March 2019 by industry-experienced IT professionals who all originated from Marawi. They all belong to a Muslim minority in the Philippines called the Meranaws (People of the Lake), which belongs to a bigger class called the Moro People (a.k.a Bangasamoro People). ITSMarawi is a local IT services, consulting and business solutions organization that delivers affordable, relevant and industry-standard IT solutions to various institutions. They provide solutions to expensive and/or inefficient institutional processes, transforming these institutions to achieve their full potential. With its team composed of industry-experienced project managers, architects and developers, along with its own consultants from the academe, ITSMarawi promises real results and ensures level of security and integrity no other local firm can match.

Vision

ITSMarawi aspires to be the leading IT institution that will help transform the BARMM region into an IT competitive institution both locally and globally.

Mission

To promote the IT industry in the BARMM Region and in turn develop the IT competitiveness of the Bangasamoro People thru job creations and opportunities for the locals.

To provide affordable, relevant and industry-standard IT solutions to both private and government institutions in the BARMM region.
